Meet the Phantom: The most hated mob in game

The story behind the creature that won the mob vote and how it quickly fell to be the most disliked mob in game

Learn More

Phantoms were one of four choices in the Minecon Earth 2017 mob vote

The "Monster of the Ocean Depths" was meant to spawn in deep waters, it would use its tongue-like tentacle to pull you down and drown you. It was meant to bring more life to oceans.

The "Monster of the Night Skies" or as we now know it as the Phantom, was meant to be attracted by insomnia, fly around its prey and swoop down to attack its target. It was meant to be one of the few flying mobs that can spawn on the Overworld

The "Great Hunger" was a cute-looking mob with a big mouth that would sink into the ground and hide, and would consume the enchantments from items or other mobs that happen to walk through its jaws.

The Hovering Inferno was the fourth mob you could vote for, it was a more dangerous version of the Blaze and would be a strange encounter that would happen in the Nether, acting like some sort of mini-boss. It was meant to make the nether more scary and exciting.

The life of a phantom is literally as valueless as a summer ant. It is a worthless, utter tumor of the sky. I have had to rescue too many inventories from people who passed out while out. The freedom of phantoms is an insult to me, I'd have them all in zoos
- GhostOfStarman, Minerealm
